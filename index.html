About Kitt Franco-Belge

The Kitt Franco-Belge project is dedicated to preserving and promoting the unique heritage and culture associated with the Kitt breed, known for its intelligence and companionship.

Join us in our mission to highlight the significance of Kitt within the Franco-Belgian cultural landscape.
